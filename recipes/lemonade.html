Lemonade

  • 1 3/4 cups of white sugar
  • 1 cup of water
  • 9 lemons
  • 7 cups of cold water
  • ice as desired

  1. Combine sugar and 1 cup water in a small saucepan. Stirring as mixture comes to a boil. Set aside to cool
  2. Roll lemons on counter to soften. Cut lemons in half and squeeze juice into a liquid measuring cup
  3. Pour 7 cups of water into a pitcher with ice and add in the lemon juice and simple syrup.
