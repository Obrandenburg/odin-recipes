Baked Chicken

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 Tbs. olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp. coarse sea salt
  • 1 pinch Creole seasoning
  • 1 Tbs. chicken broth

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Rub chicken with olive oil and sprinkle both sides with seasalt and Creole seasoning. Place in a broiler pan
  3. Bake in oven for 10 min. Flip chicken and cook 15 more minutes, or until juices run clear
  4. Move chicken to a plate
  5. Add chicken broth to the boiler plate to scrape off any browned bits that remain.
  6. Drizzle chicken with the pan sauce and serve
